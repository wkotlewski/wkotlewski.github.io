ABOUT

Whitney is a Senior User Experience Architect working at Esri's corporate headquarters in Redlands, CA. Her core responsibilities are to support product teams, for mobile, desktop and web applications, with designs that embrace UX Principals and User Research.

Whitney’s most influential work at Esri has been her contributions to Esri’s flagship data visualization application, Insights for ArcGIS, where she led the UX design of the products current interface.

Today, Whitney has impacted various new projects at Esri and has recently completed the redesign of a mobile navigation application for professionals in the field. Not only is she a designer at Esri but is currently pursuing a Ph.D. at Claremont Graduate University. In addition, she is the the visionary and co-founder of Black Girls M.A.P.P.; a community focused group to empower women of color in the field of GIS. Her vision through this initiative is to inspire a network of women of color, using GIS to solve community problems.

Navigation App

Navigator for ArcGIS: iOS and Android

Navigator for ArcGIS is a professional mobile navigation app for Android and iOS that uses GIS software to route your field workforce. It comes with hundreds of global maps, or you can add your own custom map. Define travel modes, route on your own roads, and search your assets.
